How You Can Help

Donate: Your financial support helps us provide essential services to the children in our care.

Volunteer: Join our team and make a direct impact by volunteering your time and skills.

Sponsor a Child: By sponsoring a child, you can help cover the costs of their education, healthcare, and daily needs.

At Sheillah Children Foundation, we believe that together, we can bring hope and build dreams for orphaned children in the slums of Kampala.

Sheillah Childrens Foundation

Sheillah Children Foundation is a non-profit, non-government organization working with orphaned children in Uganda. Sheillah Children Foundation was established as a community-based organization in 2019 and later registered as an NGO in 2024 with functional governance structures including a five-member board and, a senior management team.
